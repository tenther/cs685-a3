The first detail image show the track of the four robots meeting at the first obstacle and and working around it. It's a little hard to make out the robots. They are the small colored dots. The second detail image show the goal with all robots arriving in roughly the same place. They don't meet in exactly the same place because the program stops with the robot is with 0.1 m of the goal. The vector at this point are less distinct because their intensity is proportional to the distance from the goal.

The third show the how two robots working around the southern edge of the second obstacle. You can see how there motion is jerky as the robot move from a potential field where the repulsion is zero, to where it is strong within 5 m of the edge of the obstacle.
